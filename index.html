Our Vision and Mission

"If you cut a tree, you kill a life.
  If you save a tree, you save a life.
  If you plant a tree, you plant a life."

To provide a better world for our future generations. To provide them with abundant of resources so that they don't have to suffer for common things which we are wasting today.

Why Tree Plantation ?

"The best time to plant a tree is just now"

Tree plantation is not a need but a necessity. As tree plantation plays an important role in our economic development and environmental balance, we should plant more and more trees. We need trees now more than ever! Trees improve air quality by producing oxygen. They also store carbon, offsetting harmful byproducts of fossil-fuel burning. To Avoid further consequences we must start planting trees RIGHT NOW.




Core Values

"Earth Provide enough to satisfy every man's need not every man's greed"

Our Key goal and objective is to plant as many trees as possible in our lifetime. By planting trees we are not only making our environment more beautiful but we are also making it more liveable.

Lets Save Planet

Lets Save Earth is a Non Profit Org. founded in 2016 based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, INDIA. There are many reasons for us to establish Save Green. Everyone knows that trees are very important part of life but after that the same humans are destroying trees all over the World in the name of infrastructure and development.
